# Our models library

Over the years, Cormas community has produced many wonderful models that are available online for the general public.
Some of those are simple models to teach agent-based modelling or demonstrate certain features of Cormas.
Others are more complex models that were created for various research projects and applied in the field in Europe, Africa, South America, or South-East Asia. These old models were developed on Cormas VisualWorks.

You can find the list of our published models on the [Cormas VW website](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/tousmodeles.htm). The models collection on Cormas VW contains _90 models_ in total. You can look at the [**complete list**](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/tousmodeles.htm) or browse them by categories:

- [Models on renewable resource management](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/ressource.htm) _(15 models)_
- [Supply chains and exchanges](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/economie.htm) _(5 models)_
- [Land-use dynamics and multiple uses](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/espace.htm) _(16 models)_
- [Application of ABM for serious games](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/jeuderole.htm) _(16 models)_
- [Theoretical models](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/theorique.htm) _(14 models)_
- [Didactic models](https://vw.cormas.org/en/applica/didactique.htm) _(19 models)_
