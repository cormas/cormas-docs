# Community

Cormas is developed and maintained by an open and interdisciplinary community of researchers, developers, and practitioners working on participatory agent-based modelling and serious games.

There are several ways to connect with us depending on your needs.

## General Contact

For institutional inquiries, partnerships, administrative matters, or general questions about the Cormas project, you can contact us at <EmailLink user="contact" domain="cormas.org" />

Development discussions are coordinated internally. If you are interested in contributing, please consult our guide on [How to Contribute to Cormas](./community/contribute).

## Modelers Community Mailing List

The Modelers mailing list is open to anyone interested in using Cormas for modelling, research, teaching, or serious games.

To subscribe: [https://lists.cormas.org/wws/subscribe/modelers](https://lists.cormas.org/wws/subscribe/modelers)

This list is intended for:

- Discussions about modelling with Cormas  
- Companion modelling and participatory simulation  
- Research questions and methodological exchange  
- Sharing experiences and use cases 

:::info Important
When you join the mailing list, we kindly ask you to introduce yourself.

Please tell us:

- Your name and affiliation  
- Your modelling background  
- How you are currently using Cormas, or how you intend to use it  

This helps us maintain a constructive and collaborative environment and allows other members to better understand your context.
:::


## Discord Server

We also host a Discord server for real-time interaction.

The Discord server complements the mailing lists. It is designed for:

- Quick technical questions  
- Informal discussion  
- Community exchange  
- Live collaboration and voice meetings  

It is the most responsive place to ask short questions or start exploratory discussions. Longer or archival discussions are usually better suited for mailing lists.
